Definition 2.1.2.

The multiple integral of a function \(f\) on the cuboid \(\mathcal{C}\) is the limit
\begin{equation*} \idotsint_\mathcal{C} f(x_1, \ldots, x_n) \, \tder{}{\textnormal{V}} = \lim_{\|\mathcal{P}\| \to 0} RS_{\mathcal{P}} \end{equation*}
if it exists. If the limit exists, we say that \(f\) is integrable on \(\mathcal{C}\text{.}\)
