Definition 1.7.2.

We say that a subspace \(X\) of \(\mathbb{R}^n\) is smooth at \(p\) if there is an open set \(U\) of \(\mathbb{R}^m\) and a smooth parameterization \(\mb{r}: U \to X\) with image equal to a ball in \(\mathbb{R}^n\) intersected with \(X\text{.}\) We say that \(X\) is smooth if it is closed and smooth at every point \(p\) in \(X\text{.}\)
