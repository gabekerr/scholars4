Definition 4.3.1.

Given a smooth oriented curve \((\mathcal{C}, \vol^{\mathcal{C}})\) in \(\mathbb{R}^n\) and a vector field \(\mb{F}\) defined at every point in \(\mathcal{C}\text{,}\) the vector line integral of \(\mb{F}\) along \(\mathcal{C}\) is the integral
\begin{equation*} \int_{\mathcal{C}} \mb{F} (\mb{r} ) \cdot \tder{}{\mb{r}} := \int_{\mathcal{C}} \mb{F}^\vee . \end{equation*}
