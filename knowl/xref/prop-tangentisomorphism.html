Proposition 1.3.9.

For any \(\mb{a}\) in an inner product space \(V\text{,}\) there is a linear isomorphism
\begin{equation*} T : V \to T_{\mb{a}} V. \end{equation*}

Proof.

Define \(T\) by taking \(T (\mb{v})\) to \(D_{\mb{v}}\text{.}\) By CorollaryÂ 1.3.7 this is a linear transformation and by the definition of \(T_{\mb{a}} V\) it is an isomorphism.
